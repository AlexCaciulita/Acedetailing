Gratis

De ce afisam intervale de pret?

Credem in transparenta totala. Pretul final depinde de doi factori:

Dimensiunea masinii

  • Mica (Smart, Mini) - multiplicator 0.85x
  • Compacta (Golf, Focus) - multiplicator 0.95x
  • Sedan (Seria 3, C-Class) - pret de baza 1.0x
  • SUV / Van (X5, GLC, Q7) - multiplicator 1.2x

Starea masinii

  • Perfecta (noua / recent detailata) - 0.9x
  • Usoara (utilizare normala) - pret de baza 1.0x
  • Medie (murdarie vizibila) - 1.1x
  • Grea (foarte murdara) - 1.25x
